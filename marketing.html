About Tiger Habits

Tiger Habits is a personal project created with passion and dedication. The goal is to help users build better daily habits through simple and effective tracking tools.

Developer Vision

Currently, the application does not include paid features or subscriptions. In the future, monetization options may be introduced to support continued development.

Value Commitment

I strive to improve the app regularly, adding thoughtful updates that genuinely help users improve their daily lives.
